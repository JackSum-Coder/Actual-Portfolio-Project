I'm Jack Sommerville, an incoming senior at Castle High School with a passion for technology, music, and building things. My interest in computer science has grown from Scratch to completing all three CS courses my school offers, while taking a rigorous schedule of AP classes and advanced math.

Outside the classroom, I competed at State with We The People, where I strengthened my civics and critical thinking skills. I'm also active in National Honor Society, Student Council, and volunteer with Sleep in Heavenly Peace, building beds for children in need.

Music has been a major part of my life for years—I play piano and drums, have performed in a band, and enjoy recording music for my youtube channel. Outside of coding and music, I spend my time at the gym, building Lego projects, hiking, or playing disc golf.
